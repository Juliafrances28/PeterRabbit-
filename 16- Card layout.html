I ❤️ Rabbits

    Carrots

    Feed a rabbit a carrot? I have always loved rabbits ever since I was a kid. My favorite well-loved childhood stuffed animal was Peter Rabbit from the story "A Tale of Peter Rabbit" by Beatrix Potter. My home is filled with rabbit items. My aunt did some paintings for my apartment. One of them is the Alice in Wonderland rabbit statue in Central Park. "I'm late, I'm late, for a very important date." On my nightstand along with some books are bunny bookends my sister gave me for my birthday this year. On my windowsill is a set of pewter rabbits I found in a knickknack store.
